HTML5 and CSS3 for the Real world Chapter 8 Notes

Key Terms

  • Transform
    • Translate - move left, right, up or down
    • Rotate - Control rotation by degress
    • Scale - increase or decrease by x and y axis
    • Skew - Skews along x and y axis
  • Transition - Allows you to change CSS properties over time
    • transition-property, transition-duration, transition-timing-function, transition-delay
  • Animations - Controlled with keyframes, like Adobe Premiere

Chapter 12 Notes

  • Canvas - Lets you draw on a 2D or 3D space
